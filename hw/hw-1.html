Why is the minimum score on an assignment in this course 50%? In your HW1 file, give your opinion or conclusion, and also explain why.

While a 50-point floor promotes inclusive pedagogy by correcting the mathematical bias of the zero and preventing "mathematical hopelessness" for students facing external crises, it risks masking a lack of foundational competency in technical fields like front-end development. Balancing this requires weighing the benefit of sustained student persistence against the potential for grade inflation and the "false floor" that might signal a level of mastery not actually present in a student's code.
